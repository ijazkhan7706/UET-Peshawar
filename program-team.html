Program Teams

The university under its Notification No. 16/12/O-II/Acad dated March 11, 2015 has appointed faculty to perform as program team members. The program teams represent fifteen (15) engineering departments at the main campus at Peshawar, and the satellite campuses at Abbottabad, Bannu, and Mardan. As per HEC Assessment Manual, the program teams are responsible to prepare the Self Assessment Report (SAR).
